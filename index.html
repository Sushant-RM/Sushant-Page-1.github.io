The course **Design and Analysis of Algorithms** is all about learning how to solve problems in the smartest way possible. Algorithms are step-by-step instructions that help computers do things like finding the shortest route on a map, sorting a list of names, or securing your online data.

In this course, we learned about **data structures** like boxes for storing data (e.g., lists, stacks, and queues) and different ways to solve problems, such as breaking a problem into smaller parts or finding the best option step by step. These are tools that make programs faster and more efficient.
